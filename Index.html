Weekly Schedule

Mon / Thu

Full Body + Core Focus

⏱ ~40 min
🔥
Warm-up: 5 min walkpad, easy pace

Exercises

Dead Bug3 × 10 each side

Mat. Slow & controlled — the best deep core activator for lower belly.

Lat Pulldown3 × 12 reps

Squeeze shoulder blades at bottom. Full range of motion.

Glute Bridge3 × 15 reps

Mat. Hold 2 sec at top. Add a resistance band above knees for extra glute burn.

Dumbbell Row (single arm)3 × 10 each side

Standing, hinge forward. Brace core, keep back flat.

Dumbbell Side Bend3 × 12 each side

Standing. Hold one dumbbell, slowly bend to side. Targets obliques & waist.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift3 × 12 reps

Standing. Hinge from hips, feel hamstrings stretch. Great for back flab area.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down: 5 min: hip flexors, hamstrings, cat-cow

Tue / Fri

Pilates + Lower Belly & Glutes

⏱ ~35 min
🔥
Warm-up: 5 min walkpad, moderate pace

Exercises

Hundred (Pilates)1 × 100 pumps

Mat. Keep low back pressed down. Best lower belly exercise there is.

Leg Circles (Pilates)3 × 10 each leg

Mat. Stabilizes hips and targets lower abs. Keep pelvis still.

Clamshell3 × 15 each side

Mat, on your side. Targets glute med — great for hip shape and stability.

Donkey Kicks3 × 15 each side

Mat, on all fours. Squeeze glute at the top. Slow and controlled.

Reverse Crunch3 × 12 reps

Mat. Lift hips off the floor — no swinging. Hits lower abs directly.

Dumbbell Side Bend3 × 12 each side

Standing. One dumbbell. Slow lateral bend — great oblique toner.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down: 5 min: child's pose, seated twist, pigeon stretch

Wed / Sat

Upper Body + Core Stability

⏱ ~40 min
🔥
Warm-up: 5 min walkpad, brisk walk

Exercises

Lat Pulldown (wide grip)3 × 12 reps

Creates a strong, defined back — transforms how you look in a dress.

Face Pull (lat machine, rope attachment)3 × 15 reps

Attach rope to lat machine at face height. Pull to forehead, elbows high. Posture + shoulder health.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press3 × 10 reps

Standing. Core braced, don't arch back. Great shoulder definition.

Dumbbell Side Bend3 × 12 each side

Standing. Slow and controlled. Waist tightener.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl3 × 12 reps

Standing. Slow on the way down — that's where the work is.

Bird Dog3 × 10 each side

Mat. Spine stability + core + glute activation. Pilates-adjacent feel.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down: 5 min: chest opener, thoracic rotation, forward fold
