Weekly Schedule

Mon / Thu

Full Body + Core Focus

⏱ ~40 min
🔥
Warm-up 5 min walkpad, easy pace

Exercises

Dead Bug 3 × 10 each side

Slow and controlled. Great for deep core activation and lower belly control.

Lat Pulldown 3 × 12 reps

Squeeze your shoulder blades at the bottom. Keep the movement smooth.

Glute Bridge 3 × 15 reps

Hold 2 seconds at the top. Add a resistance band above your knees for extra burn.

Dumbbell Row 3 × 10 each side

Brace your core, hinge forward, and keep your back flat.

Dumbbell Side Bend 3 × 12 each side

Move slowly. Think controlled waist and oblique work, not momentum.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift 3 × 12 reps

Hinge from the hips and feel your hamstrings stretch.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down 5 min: hip flexors, hamstrings, cat-cow

Tue / Fri

Pilates + Lower Belly & Glutes

⏱ ~35 min
🔥
Warm-up 5 min walkpad, moderate pace

Exercises

Hundred 1 × 100 pumps

Keep your low back pressed down. Modify with bent knees if needed.

Leg Circles 3 × 10 each leg

Keep your pelvis still. Small circles are totally fine.

Clamshell 3 × 15 each side

Targets the side glutes for hip shape and stability.

Donkey Kicks 3 × 15 each side

Squeeze at the top. Keep your hips square.

Reverse Crunch 3 × 12 reps

Lift your hips without swinging your legs.

Dumbbell Side Bend 3 × 12 each side

Slow, gentle, and controlled.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down 5 min: child’s pose, seated twist, pigeon stretch

Wed / Sat

Upper Body + Core Stability

⏱ ~40 min
🔥
Warm-up 5 min walkpad, brisk walk

Exercises

Lat Pulldown, Wide Grip 3 × 12 reps

Great for back definition and posture.

Face Pull 3 × 15 reps

Pull toward your forehead with elbows high.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 × 10 reps

Brace your core and avoid arching your back.

Dumbbell Side Bend 3 × 12 each side

Slow and controlled waist work.

Dumbbell Bicep Curl 3 × 12 reps

Control the lowering phase — that’s where the work is.

Bird Dog 3 × 10 each side

Keep your hips level. Think balance, core, and glute activation.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down 5 min: chest opener, thoracic rotation, forward fold

Sat / Sun

Cardio + Full Body Sculpt

⏱ ~45 min
🔥
Warm-up 10 min walkpad — start easy, build to brisk

Exercises

Walkpad Intervals 15 min total

2 min easy, 1 min fast, repeat.

Dumbbell Squat to Press 3 × 12 reps

Squat down, then press dumbbells overhead as you stand.

Pilates Roll-Up 3 × 10 reps

Slow and controlled. Modify with bent knees if needed.

Clamshell 3 × 15 each side

Add a band above the knees when you want to level up.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift 3 × 12 reps

Hamstrings, glutes, and back all at once.

Dead Bug 3 × 10 each side

Finish with control. Deep core stabilizer.

🧘‍♀️
Cool-down 5–10 min full stretch: hips, back, hamstrings
